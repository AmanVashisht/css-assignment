Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a library of free and open-source fonts that are available for use on websites and in other digital products. The library includes a wide variety of fonts, from classic serifs to modern sans-serifs. Google Fonts also offers a number of features that make it easy to use, such as a search bar, a preview tool, and a variety of ways to download the fonts. Google Fonts is a great resource for designers and developers who want to use beautiful and high-quality fonts on their projects.
