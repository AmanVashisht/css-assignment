About Us



PW Skills is an online learning platform that offers courses on coding, data science, and other technical skills. The platform was founded by Alakh Pandey, the founder of Physics Wallah, an online education platform. PW Skills offers a wide range of courses, from beginner to advanced levels. The courses are taught by experienced instructors and cover a variety of topics, including web development, Java, C++, data structures, and algorithms. The platform also offers a number of features that make it a valuable resource for learners, including interactive exercises, practice problems, and a job portal. PW Skills is a great option for anyone who wants to learn technical skills.
